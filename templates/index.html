Recovery Milestones

A typical knee replacement recovery timeline

🏥

Day 1

Walk with frame. Pain management begins.

🩹

Day 7

Walk 100m. Wound check. Flexion ~60°.

✂️

Day 14

Suture removal. One crutch. Walk 200m.

🚶

Week 6

90°+ flexion. May drive. Most aids removed.

🏊

Month 3

Cycling, swimming. Return to light work.

🏆

Month 12

Full function. Annual X-ray review.

Exercise Library

Evidence-based rehabilitation exercises for every stage

🦵

Ankle Pumps

Week 1+

Flex and point your foot up and down to improve circulation and prevent blood clots.

⏱ 10 reps🔁 3× daily😌 Low intensity
🏋️

Quad Sets

Week 1+

Press the back of your knee into the bed and hold for 5 seconds to activate quadriceps.

⏱ 10 reps🔁 3× daily😊 Easy
📐

Heel Slides

Week 1+

Slide your heel toward your buttock to gently improve knee flexion range of motion.

⏱ 10 reps🔁 2× daily😊 Easy
🚴

Stationary Cycling

Week 6+

Low-impact cycling to improve knee flexion, muscle strength and cardiovascular fitness.

⏱ 10–20 min🔁 Daily💪 Moderate
🌊

Hydrotherapy

Week 6+

Water-based exercises reduce joint load while building strength and improving mobility.

⏱ 30 min🔁 2× week💪 Moderate
⬇️

Mini Squats

Week 8+

Shallow squats to 45° strengthen quads and glutes, improving functional movement.

⏱ 10 reps🔁 2× daily🔥 Challenging
